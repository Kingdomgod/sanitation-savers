Collect and sort recyclables anywhere

At home

Start collecting recyclables at home by finding out which packaging types your collector or drop-off sites will take and by designating a special area for your recyclables to stay. Get everyone in the family on board, and it will become second nature.

At work

Promote recycling at the office by setting up a recycling scheme in the office. Start by getting top management on board, communicating with employees and placing a separate bin for recyclables in easy to access areas. Most people are willing once someone leads the way.

At school

Schools are great places to start collecting recyclables. Get your principal involved, get the message out there and invest in a few large bins, so learners can easily separate recyclables from waste. Encourage everyone to get involved and contribute to a cleaner and greener school environment.
